Covid-19 Sentiment Analysis

Our hypothesis is that sentiment toward the Covid-19 vaccination program has been improving over the months since the announcement of the vaccine and FDA Emergency Use Authorization approvals of the first three vaccines. By analyzing tweets by keyword and utlizing sentiment anaylysis, we will deploy an app that will allow users to choose a date, or range of dates, to gain an understanding of the changing nature of the U.S.-based Twitter users attitudes toward Covid-19 vaccines.
